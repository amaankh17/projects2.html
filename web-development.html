Web Development

Web development refers to the creating, building, and maintaining of websites. It includes aspects such as web design, web publishing, web programming, and database management. It is the creation of an application that works over the internet i.e. websites.Web developer skills are often applied to the non-design aspects of building websites writing markup and coding. Web developers may also use content management systems (CMS) to make content changes easier for other developers or end-users who may lack technical skills.
